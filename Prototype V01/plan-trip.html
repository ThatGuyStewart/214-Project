Onboard

Fly DreamAir have many onboard food and drink options to make your travel comfortable
and easy. Select from snacks, meals, and a vast selection of juices, soft drinks, and
alcoholic beverages. These are available in different packages.

These packages can easily be added when you book your flight with Fly DreamAir or
by managing your booking with us at any time.

Additional drinks can be purchased at any time onboard your flight.
Packages

Package One
Snack and a drink.

Perfect for a shorter journey or an overnight flight.


Package Two
Snack, meal and two drinks.

Perfect for a long flight.


Package Three
Snack, two meals and three drinks.

Perfect for a journey with connecting flights.
